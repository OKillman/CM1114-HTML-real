Welcome to the exciting world of mixology at RGU! Whether you're a seasoned cocktail enthusiast or just starting to explore the art of crafting delicious drinks, you've come to the right place. Our Mixology Society is dedicated to bringing together students who share a passion for creativity, flavor, and the joy of mixing drinks. Join us as we embark on a journey to discover new techniques, experiment with unique ingredients, and perfect classic recipes. From mocktails to craft cocktails, there's something for everyone to enjoy and learn. Throughout the year, we'll host engaging workshops, fun social events, and even competitions to showcase your mixology skills. It's not just about the drinks; it's about the camaraderie and the shared love for the craft. So, whether you're here to expand your knowledge, meet like-minded individuals, or simply have a good time, we invite you to become part of our vibrant mixology community. Cheers to new friendships and unforgettable flavors!
